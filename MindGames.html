Overview

Welcome to MindGames. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our brain-training mobile application.

By using MindGames, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • Display your name and scores on the global leaderboards
  • Save and sync your best scores across sessions and devices
  • Show your personal profile with game statistics
  • Authenticate you securely via Firebase Authentication
  • Prevent duplicate entries on leaderboards

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purposes.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for commercial purposes.

Your data may be disclosed only in the following limited cases:

  • Leaderboards: Your display name and scores are visible to all app users on the public leaderboard
  • Legal requirement: If required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Safety: To protect the rights and safety of users or the public

⚠️ Leaderboard visibility: When you sign in with Google, your name appears on the public leaderboard. Guest users appear as "Guest" with no identifiable information.

🗃️

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

  • Google Sign-In accounts: Data retained until you request deletion
  • Guest accounts: Anonymous data is retained indefinitely but contains no personal information
  • Game scores: Retained to maintain leaderboard history

Upon account deletion request, all personally identifiable data will be removed within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • ✅ Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • ✅ Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • ✅ Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • ✅ Opt-out: Play as Guest to avoid any personal data collection
  • ✅ Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

MindGames is rated Everyone (E) and is suitable for all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent.

Children under 13 should use the Guest mode, which requires no personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal data without consent, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Security Rules: Users can only read/write their own data
  • No passwords stored: Authentication handled entirely by Google
  • Minimal collection: We only collect what is strictly necessary

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
