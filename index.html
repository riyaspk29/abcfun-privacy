Privacy Policy

ABC Fun! — Kids Alphabet Learning App

📅 Effective Date: April 12, 2025

🛡️ Our Commitment to Your Child's Privacy

ABC Fun! is a safe, educational app designed for children aged 2–6. We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy and safety of young users. This Privacy Policy explains clearly what data we collect (spoiler: almost none), how the app works, and your rights as a parent or guardian.

✅ ABC Fun! does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information from children.

📋 Information We Collect

ABC Fun! is designed to work fully offline with no user accounts required.

  • No name, email, or contact details are collected.
  • No location data is collected or used.
  • No photos, microphone, or camera access is requested.
  • No device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID) are tracked.
  • No usage analytics or behavioural tracking is performed.

The app uses your device's built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine solely to pronounce letters and words aloud. This happens entirely on-device and no audio data is sent to any server.

👶 Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

ABC Fun! is directed to children under the age of 13. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • We do not display third-party advertisements.
  • We do not include social features, chat, or external links accessible to children.
  • We do not use in-app purchases.
  • All content is age-appropriate and educational.

🔗 Third-Party Services

ABC Fun! does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, social media plugins, or data brokers.

The app uses the following device-native features only:

  • Text-to-Speech (expo-speech) — on-device, no data sent externally.
  • Audio playback (expo-av) — plays bundled music files locally.

No data is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.

🔐 App Permissions

ABC Fun! requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • INTERNET — Required by the React Native framework. The app does not use the internet for any functionality.

No permissions for camera, microphone, contacts, location, or storage are requested.

🔒 Data Security

Since ABC Fun! does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no personal data at risk. All app content (letters, sounds, music) is bundled within the app and stored locally on your device.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review what data (if any) has been collected about your child.
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your child.
  • Refuse further collection of data from your child.

Since we collect no personal data, exercising these rights simply means uninstalling the app from your device, which removes all local data.

📝 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage parents to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of ABC Fun! after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
