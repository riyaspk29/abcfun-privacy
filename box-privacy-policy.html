Privacy Policy

Boxbreak by AppCraft Studio · Last updated: 4 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Boxbreak mobile application (the “App”) handles information when you use it. The App is published by AppCraft Studio (“we”, “us”, “our”).

By installing or using the App, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

Boxbreak does not require an account. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not operate servers that collect data from the App.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves game progress (such as scores, current level, and settings) on your device using your operating system’s local storage (shared_preferences). This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC. AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain data to deliver and measure ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • Device and operating system information
  • Interaction data with ads shown in the App

Google’s use of this information is governed by Google’s own privacy policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads .

On Android, you can reset or limit ad personalization through Settings → Google → Ads. On iOS, you can manage tracking permissions through Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through advertising networks integrated into the App, please contact us so we can work with the relevant provider to address it.

Permissions

The App requests the INTERNET permission on Android, which is required to load advertisements. No other sensitive permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage) are requested.

Data Retention

Local game data is retained on your device until you clear the App’s data or uninstall the App. We do not retain any personal data on our own servers because we do not collect any.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete. For data collected by Google AdMob, please use the controls described above or contact Google directly.
