Privacy Policy for Tapix

Last updated: 23 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Tapix" mobile application ("the App", "we", "our", "us"), published by RiyasApps, handles information when you use it. By installing or using Tapix you agree to the terms below.

1. Information we collect

Tapix itself does not ask you for any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or submit your name, email, phone number, or address.

The following data is handled:

  • On-device data. Your game high scores and preferences are saved locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.
  • Advertising data (via Google AdMob). Tapix displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data such as:
    • Advertising ID (a resettable identifier provided by Android)
    • Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
    • Approximate (non-precise) location derived from IP address
    • App interaction events needed to serve and measure ads
    This processing is performed by Google, not by us. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Advertising Policies and the Google Privacy Policy .

2. How the data is used

Data collected by AdMob is used to show you ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud and abuse, and — depending on your consent and region — to show personalized advertising. We do not sell your data.

3. Children's privacy

Tapix is suitable for general audiences but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

4. Your choices

  • Reset or limit ad tracking. On Android, go to Settings → Privacy → Ads (or Settings → Google → Ads) to reset your Advertising ID or delete it to opt out of personalized ads.
  • Uninstall. Uninstalling Tapix removes all locally stored data, including high scores.

5. Data retention

We do not operate any server that stores your personal data. Any data processed by AdMob is retained by Google according to its own policies.

6. Permissions

Tapix requests only the permissions needed to display ads (internet access and network state). It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, photos, or files.
